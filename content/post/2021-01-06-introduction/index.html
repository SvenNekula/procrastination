Who am I?

My name is Sven Nekula, I’m 23 years old and I graduated from the Friedrich-Alexander- University in the summer of 2020 with a Bachelor of Arts in Economics and Sociology. Since November 2020 I’ve been studying Survey Statistics at the Otto-Friedrich-University in Bamberg. The first time I came into contact with statistics was in the third semester of my bachelor studies. I quickly realized, that I enjoyed statistics the most out of all my classes and thus decided to attend more and more classes that dealt with statistics. In the fourth semester I took classes that taught the use of SPSS and R and I’ve worked with R ever since. In my fifth and sixth semester I came across GitHub and rmarkdown and created two small websites that you can see on my GitHub profile.
